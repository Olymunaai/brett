With our roots going back to 1970, we are a well-established and respected law firm with a focus on client care. Regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and CQS Accredited, we pride ourselves on providing a quality service to all of our clients.

Legal services can be obtained in a number of different ways. A variety of organisations, both large and small, offer all kinds of service. Virtual internet firms and large new market entrants may offer very low cost products but with little contact with your lawyer and no face to face advice. City centre firms may provide a good service but at a higher price.

Whoever you choose to provide your legal service you need to be comfortable with the cost and the standard of service provided. At Bridgeman Kettle LLP we ensure that each client has the devoted attention of a very experienced senior lawyer who will no doubt remember you and your case in the future.
