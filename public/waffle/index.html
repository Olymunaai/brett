Below are some examples of what to expect.

Mac, Waff & Cheese has become an instant classic. It's a beautiful marriage between the highly regarding Mac & Cheese, built on a foundation of potato waffles. A light seasoning of Black Pepper and a gentle topping of grated cheese really is the cherry on the cake.

The Burgwaff is an assortment of flavours, a playground for the pallet, a construction for the adventurous. While exploration here is seemingly infinite, it starts with a basic combination of mature cheddar, a succulent beef patty and delicately battered onion rings atop a potato waffle. With a drizzle of lidl's own brown sauce, this is one for the burger lovers.

Chilli Cheese Waffles is exactly as you'd expect. It's simple and incredibly effective. The secret here is in the simmering of the chilli. Let it take in all of those delicious flavours in order to properly compliment the crispiness of those elegant browning waffles. This dish is a concerto. The chilli and cheese are the melody and the waffle-base is the percussion, driving the tempo and the rhythm.
